Our Marketing Account is our next service level and it allows you to send out push notifications and emails to your business followers, and users who recommended and liked your business. Sport Social also supplies complete analytics on the number Of users visiting your site etc, the cost Of this account is $100 a month. As we are just launching in your market we are offering a 60 day trial for either Of our paid plans, you just have to add your credit card information to be able to start using it but it won't be billed unless you want to keep the paid account after the 60 day trial period
